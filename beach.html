Coastal Clean-Up of Cangmating Beach


Date: October 12 2025 | 05:00AM

Organizer: Friend of the Philippine Blue Coast

Event Type: Community

Points: [3] Assistant Organizer

What

So What

Now What

The Coastal Clean-Up at Cangmating Beach was on October 12, 2025, at 5:00 AM. We gathered early to clean up trash and debris along the shoreline as part of a community service event. I joined with my friends, and we were assigned to collect plastics, bottles, and other waste to help restore the beach’s cleanliness.

Waking up early wasn’t easy, but once we got there, it was actually fun. The beach was quiet and peaceful, and cleaning it up with my friends made it feel less like work. I realized how much trash people leave behind and how bad it can get if no one does anything. The most satisfying part was seeing the beach look cleaner after we were done. It made me feel like we actually did something useful.

This event made me more conscious about the environment and how small actions like this can have a big impact. I learned that teamwork really matters when you’re working toward something meaningful. I can apply this by being more mindful about waste and joining more clean-up drives. If I could do it again, I’d bring more gloves and snacks, but yeah, I’d totally join again.

Because of this activity, I am more environmentally aware and motivated to take care of nature.
