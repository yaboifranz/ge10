Basketball Pasiklaban


Date: October 22, 2025 | 02:00PM

Organizer: Silliman University Senior High School

Event Type: Court

Points: [1] Spectator

What

So What

Now What

The Basketball Pasiklaban took place on October 22, 2025, at 2:00 PM. It was also part of the SHS Sportsfest. My friends and I watched together as different teams competed against each other. The whole place had that loud, energetic sports vibe, and everyone was cheering for their favorites.

Even though I was just watching, it was fun seeing how competitive the players were. Basketball games always bring out the excitement in the crowd. I expected it to be just another game, but the players were actually really good. The best part was hanging out with my friends and just enjoying the atmosphere. The hardest part was that it got a little crowded, but it was fine overall. It reminded me how fun it is to be part of events like that even if you’re not the one playing.

Watching the games made me appreciate how sports bring people together. I learned that you don’t have to be an athlete to enjoy sports events. I can apply that by being more supportive and showing up to cheer for school events. The benefit of this kind of activity is that it promotes teamwork and school pride. If I could do it again, I’d probably bring a louder voice for cheering.

Because of this activity, I am more appreciative of how sports bring students together.
